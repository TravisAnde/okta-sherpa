Creating Demo Organization

10-15 minutes

Section Prerequisites

  • Access to CL03 machine
  • Your Okta Partner Portal credentials
  • Access to your business email
  • Modern web browser (Chrome or Firefox recommended)
  • Internet connectivity
Before starting: Ensure you have your Okta Partner Portal credentials ready. You will also receive demo organization credentials via email after creation.
  1. 1
    Access Demo Portal

    On CL03, open your browser and navigate to:

    https://demo.okta.com/
    Bookmark this URL for future access to the demo portal.
  2. 2
    Partner Portal Authentication

    Click "Continue with Okta Partners"

    Authentication Methods:

    Option 1: Standard Password Login
    1. Enter your Okta Partner username
    2. Enter your password
    3. Click "Sign In"
    Option 2: FastPass Authentication (if enabled)
    1. Click "Sign in with Okta FastPass"
    2. Complete the verification prompt
  3. 3
    Access Demo Creation

    On the demo portal dashboard:

    Locate "Workforce Identity" section
    Click "In just one click launch a new hands on Workforce Identity demo"
  4. 4
    Initialize Demo Organization

    Click "Create" to begin the demo organization setup

    Important: After clicking Create, you will receive an email with your demo organization credentials. Keep an eye on your inbox.
  5. 5
    Configure Demo Settings
    When prompted for additional options, select "None"
    Click "Apply" to confirm your settings
    Basic settings are sufficient for this implementation guide.
  6. 6
    Launch Your Demo

    Click "Launch" to access your new demo environment

    Check your email for demo organization credentials, which include:
    • Your unique demo organization URL
    • Administrator username
    • Temporary password
  7. 7
    Access Demo Organization

    Using the credentials from your email:

    1
    Navigate to your demo organization URL
    2
    Log in with the provided administrator credentials
    3
    Change your temporary password when prompted

Verification Checklist

Common Issues

Issue Solution
Partner Portal login issues
Verify credentials or contact partner support
Demo credentials email not received
  • Check spam folder
  • Wait 5 minutes
  • Restart process if needed
Unable to access demo organization
Ensure you're using the correct URL and credentials from the email
Password change fails
Verify new password meets complexity requirements

Proceeding to Next Section

Before moving on, ensure you have:

  • Saved your demo organization URL
  • Documented your admin credentials
  • Successfully accessed the admin console
  • Changed your temporary password
