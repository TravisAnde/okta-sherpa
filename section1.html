Creating Demo Organization

⏱️ 15-20 minutes

Section Prerequisites

  • Access to CL03 machine
  • Business email address
  • Chrome or Firefox browser
  • Internet connectivity
Before starting: Ensure you have access to your business email as you'll receive important verification links.
  1. Access Okta Partner Portal

    On CL03, open your browser and navigate to:

    https://partners.okta.com
    Bookmark this URL - you'll need to access the partner portal frequently during implementation.
    Partner Portal Login Page [Screenshot placeholder showing the login interface]
  2. Partner Portal Navigation

    After logging in, locate the Demo Organization creation area:

    1. Look for the "Demo & Development" section in the main navigation
    2. Click on "Create Demo Org" button
    If you don't see these options, ensure you have the correct partner portal permissions.
  3. Configure Organization Details

    Fill in the required information:

    Field Description Example
    Organization Name Use format: [Company]-Demo-[Date] Acme-Demo-20240428
    Admin Email Your business email address admin@company.com
    Region Choose closest geographical location US / EMEA / APAC
    Use Case Select Workforce Identity Workforce Identity
    Organization names must be unique across all Okta tenants globally.
  4. Account Activation

    Complete the activation process:

    1. Check your business email for the activation link
    2. Click the activation link (valid for 24 hours)
    3. Set up your administrator password following these requirements:
      • At least 8 characters
      • At least one uppercase letter
      • At least one lowercase letter
      • At least one number
      • At least one special character
    4. Configure security questions and answers
    Store your admin credentials securely - you'll need them throughout the implementation.
  5. Initial Console Access

    Access your new Okta tenant:

    1. Note your Okta domain: https://your-org.okta.com
    2. Log in with your admin credentials
    3. Bookmark your admin console URL
    Your Okta URL will be in the format: https://company-demo.okta.com

Verification Checklist

  • Successfully logged into Partner Portal
  • Created demo organization
  • Received activation email
  • Set up admin password
  • Configured security questions
  • Can access Okta admin console
  • Bookmarked important URLs

Common Issues

Issue Solution
No activation email received Check spam folder, verify email address, or request new activation link
Organization name already taken Try a different organization name with unique identifier
Activation link expired Return to partner portal and request new demo organization
